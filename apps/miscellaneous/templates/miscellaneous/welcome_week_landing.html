Anisoc Quiz Night

18:00 in RIL.G.03

Join us for an anime (& manga) themed quiz night and test your mettle to see if you really know your stuff! Team up with friends or find a group to participate in a range of rounds, from trivia, AMQs' and more! There are prizes at stake for winners and runners-up so you don't want to miss out!
