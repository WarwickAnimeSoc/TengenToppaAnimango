Wednesday 1st October

Movie Showing - Redline

18:00 in MS.02

Redline is about the biggest and most deadly racing tournament in the universe. Only held once every five years, everyone wants to stake their claim to fame, including JP, a reckless dare-devil driver oblivious to speed limits with his ultra-customized car - all the while, organized crime and militaristic governments want to leverage the race to their own ends. Amongst the other elite rival drivers in the tournament, JP falls for the alluring Sonoshee - but will she prove his undoing, or can a high speed romance survive a mass destruction race?

Thursday 2nd October

Anisoc Quiz Night

18:00 in LIB2

Join us for a fun filled quiz and be ready to test your knowledge of anime and manga through a gauntlet of various quiz rounds! Prizes will be up for grabs for the best performers! This will also be a great opportunity to meet other members and get to know each other!

Saturday 4th October

Games Social

13:00 - 22:00(Drop in) in OC0.04

Join us in a relaxed and informal setting to meet new people, while we host a wide variety of physical and digital games for you to enjoy! You can also take part in our mini drawing activity - bring your own art materials or use provided ones to draw your favourite characters. No experience required - veterans and novices alike can participate, with prizes up for grabs!


Feel free to just drop in for an opportunity to meet new people at the society and chat in a social environment!
